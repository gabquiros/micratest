ABOUT THE SERIES

Presented by JD Motorsport Organization in collaboration with Nissan Canada, the Nissan Micra® Cup consists of 10 races across 5 weekends in Quebec. From May to September, over 25 Nissan Micras® will star in this series, unique to Quebec and Canada.

Each racing weekend will include a 30-minute trial, a 30-minute qualifying session and two 30-minute races - that's over 2 hours of track-time, adrenaline and fun.
